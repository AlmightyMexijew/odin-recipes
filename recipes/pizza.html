Big Plate-o-Pizza

Big Plate-o-Pizza 4 cheese pizza

Description

This is Plato's Big Plate-o-Pizza. A cheese pizza that can satisfy even the hunger of the gods. In this pizza are 4 delicious kinds of cheese and the finest sauces to ever grace a pizza. It can feed no less than 4 people.

Ingredients:

  • Dough- made from 1 1/2 cups water and 3 3/4 cups flour mixed together
  • Yeast- 1 package of yeast, usually 2 - 2 1/4 teaspoons
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil- 2 tablespoons
  • Salt- 2 teaspoons
  • Sugar- 1 teaspoon
  • Cheese- 4 kinds: Fontina, Parmesan, Mozarella, Feta- to taste. No less than 8 ounces per cheese, preferably more.
