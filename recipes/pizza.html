Big Plate-o-Pizza

Big Plate-o-Pizza 4 cheese pizza

Description

This is Plato's Big Plate-o-Pizza. A cheese pizza that can satisfy even the hunger of the gods. In this pizza are 4 delicious kinds of cheese and the finest sauces to ever grace a pizza. It can feed no less than 4 people.

Ingredients:

  • Dough- made from 1 1/2 cups water and 3 3/4 cups flour mixed together
  • Yeast- 1 package of yeast, usually 2 - 2 1/4 teaspoons
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil- 2 tablespoons
  • Salt- 2 teaspoons
  • Sugar- 1 teaspoon
  • Sauce- 1/2 cup of tomato basil sauce
  • Cheese- 4 kinds: Fontina, Parmesan, Mozarella, Feta- to taste. No less than 8 ounces per cheese, preferably more.

Steps to make the pizza

  1. Make mixture of yeast and bowl of warm water
  2. Let it sit for 5 minutes
  3. Add flour,salt,sugar,and olive oil together
  4. Stir dough in the bowl for approximately 7-10 minutes until consistency is sticky to the touch
  5. Shake dough out to a clean surface, coat bowl with olive oil, then return dough inside to sit for 1 1/2 hours covered in a warm place.
  6. After dough has risen divide it according to your baking dish requirements. If you are baking a single pizza you do not need to divide it, however if you are making multiple pizzas you will need to make sure you have enough between them.
  7. Dust your hands with flour. Uncover the dough and make it into a ball. For multiple pizzas you will have multiple balls of dough
  8. Let the ball(s) sit for 15 minutes.
  9. Flatten the ball(s) on lightly flour-covered surface. Your goal here is to flatten and stretch the dough to the size of your baking tray. Keep in mind that deeper pizzas require more dough and will have less stretch to give in the process. We want to have a fairly even distribution around the baking tray.
  10. Pinch the edges to form a crust. If you wish to stuff it, this is where you place your stuffing in a thin row on the border of the pizza before rolling inward toward the center.
  11. Press lightly around making dents to prevent bubbling.
  12. Spread the cheese around in uniform layers to make things even. Don't go too crazy or it will get soggy.
  13. Slide into oven and bake at 475*F. Your goal is to aim for melted cheese and golden crust color. This takes approximately 10 to 15 minutes.
  14. Remove from oven and place somewhere to cool. Eat when you feel like it. Enjoy.
