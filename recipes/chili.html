Mama's Chili recipe

Description:

Mama's chili is the best chili ever. On a cold windy day there will always be room for Mama's chili.

Even on a hot summer day, it goes great with una cerveza, a beer and some fresh cornbread or tortilla.

So what does mama put in there anyway? Ingredients:

    Here are a list of must-have ingredients:
  • Beans. You must have at least 3 kinds of beans. I'm serious. The best kind would be kidney beans, black beans, and kidney beans.
  • Chili pepper. Diced up fine. Preferably dried but fresh does the trick.
  • Garlic. Lots of garlic. If your house doesn't smell like Gilroy,California you are doing something wrong!
  • Masa. You can substitute oatmeal or wheat flour if you cannot find this special corn flour.
  • Four diced tomatoes or 2 cans of tomato sauce.
  • Spicy paprika.
  • Sweet paprika.
  • Cumin.
  • Meat. Any cut will do so long as they are bite-sized chunks.

Steps:

    These are the steps to making Mama's Chili:
  1. Get a big pot.
  2. Soak your beans in the pot for 8 hours under cover of water.
  3. Pour out the water while retaining the beans.
  4. Fill the pot with water until about 80% submerged.
  5. Toss in the chili pepper. If you are using crushed or powdered chili then throw about 2 Tbsp in.
  6. Toss in garlic. Preferably use fresh cloves but powder is also okay.
  7. Toss in Tbsp of each paprika, and 2 Tbsp. of Cumin.
  8. Toss in tomatoes or tomato products.
  9. Toss in meat.
  10. Toss in half Tbsp of Masa.
  11. Heat, covered, on high for an hour with only enough air to prevent bubbling.
  12. After an hour, reduce heat to simmer and cover fully
  13. Stir every 10 minutes for the next hour until texture and density is sauce-like. Add water or masa as needed.
  14. Serve in bowls with tortilla, add cheese if you desire, and crack yourself a beer. you earned it.
