Greener and Leaner

Green Smoothie

Description

The Greener and Leaner smoothie will keep you fabulous and trim. It tastes great and blows the red smoothie and the blue smoothie out of the water

Ingredients

    These are the ingredients:
  • 3/4 cup of fresh pineapple juice
  • 1/2 cup of fresh spinach leaves
  • 1/4 pear chopped up
  • 1/4 green apple chopped up
  • 1/4 avocado chopped up
  • 3 florets of broccoli

Steps to make it

  1. Start by chopping each solid ingredient(pear,apple,avocado)
  2. Toss ingredients into the blender
  3. Blend thoroughly for 2 minutes
  4. Shake to free up the blender blade
  5. Blend again for 30 seconds
  6. Serve fresh in glasses

Optional Step: Add ice to blending process
